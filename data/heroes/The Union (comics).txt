'''The Union''' is a fictional [[United Kingdom|UK]] [[superhero]] team that appear in [[Comic book|comic books]] published by Marvel.<ref>{{cite web|accessdate=2020-06-08|title='The Union' Introduces New Superteam to Marvel's Comic Book Universe This May|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/union-introduces-new-superteam-marvels-comic-book-universe-may-1280051|website=The Hollywood Reporter}}</ref>

The Union has both old and new superheroes within it, including British hero [[Union Jack (Joseph Chapman)|Union Jack]] and a major new character Britannia who leads the new team in its fight against the enemies they encounter.

The Union appears in the crossover event called ''[[King in Black]]'' alongside other well known comic book heroes and teams, as well as facing off against known foes and new foes not yet fought by any Marvel superhero or team.

==Characters==
The Union was formed after discovering that an intergalactic invasion threatened to destroy the galaxy. The superhero team consists of five members; four of them representing the nations of the UK, and the fifth representing the UK as a whole.<ref name="cbr">{{Cite web|last=Adams|first=Tim|date=February 14, 2020|title=Marvel Introduces The Union, The UK's Brand-New Superhero Team|url=https://www.cbr.com/marvel-comics-the-union-united-kingdom-superhero-team/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=|website=Comic Book Resources}}</ref>

'''Britannia''' 

Britannia is the leader and represents the [[United Kingdom|UK]] superhero team. She is a powerful warrior with long black hair, and she is dressed in a shining rich dark and light contrasting blue outfit, a regal red cloak and she wields a sword and a lion shield.

'''Kelpie''' 

Kelpie represents [[Scotland]] in the superhero team. She is a powerful warrior with pink hair, with a pale light and dark contrasting blue outfit and has the power to control the ocean.

'''The Choir'''

The Choir represents [[Wales]] in the superhero team, She has black hair with a bright green strip across the top, and she is dressed in a glowing green and black contrasting outfit with a blue cloak and she wields a dagger. Her power lies within her voice, using it to attack opponents.

'''Snakes'''

Snakes represent [[Northern Ireland|N.Ireland]] in the superhero team. He is dressed in an ash and light grey contrasting tunic with his face covered, light grey trousers, black gloves and a black belt. He appears to communicate telepathically.

'''[[Union Jack (Joseph Chapman)|Union Jack]]'''

Union Jack represents [[England]] in the superhero team. He wears a shiny black mask that covers his face, and a glimmering Union Jack top and shiny black trousers and he wields a pistol. He is only pre-existing character, being the latest man to bear the name of a character who first appeared in ''[[Invaders (comics)|The Invaders #7]]'' and has since had two solo mini series.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Union Jack|url=http://www.toonopedia.com/ujack.htm|last=Marksteinâ€™s|first=Don|date=2 April 2020|website=Toonpedia|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=}}</ref>
