'''Bron-Char''' is a fictional character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]]. Created by writer [[Mark Waid]] and artist [[Joe Kubert]], the character debuted in ''[[The Avengers (comic book)|Avengers]]'' Vol. 1, #364 (July 1993). He was a member of the Lunatic Legion. He destroyed one of [[Captain America's shield]]s.

The character was portrayed by [[Rune Temte]] in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]] film ''[[Captain Marvel (film)|Captain Marvel]]'' (2019).

==Publication history==
The character first appeared in ''[[The Avengers (comic book)|Avengers]]'' Vol. 1, #364 (July 1993), and was created by writer [[Mark Waid]] and artist [[Joe Kubert]]. He was introduced as part of the Lunatic Legion during the Live, Kree or Die story arc.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://screenrant.com/captain-marvel-movie-starforce-members-confirmed/|title=Captain Marvel Brings Starforce To The MCU - Here Are All 6 Members|last=Bacon|first=Thomas|website=[[Screen Rant]]|date=March 7, 2019|accessdate=July 2, 2019|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190426154455/https://screenrant.com/captain-marvel-movie-starforce-members-confirmed/|archivedate=April 26, 2019}}</ref>

==Fictional character biography==
Bron Char was with the second lineup of the [[Lunatic Legion]] led by [[Galen Kor]] when they attacked the Cape Canaveral base, and he ambushed [[Captain America]] and smashed the triangular shield that he was carrying at the time. He battered Captain America initially, but when Captain America saw how many people the Kree had been experimenting on, he became furious and beat Bron Char badly.<ref>''[[The Avengers (comic book)|Avengers]]'' Vol. 1, #364 (July 1993). Marvel Comics.</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Bron-Char has considerable strength for a Kree.
